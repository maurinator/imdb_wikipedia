Bon Voyage, Charlie Brown (and Don't Come Back!!) (1980)

Summaries

Charlie Brown, Linus, Peppermint Patty, and Marcie travel to France as foreign exchange students. Also along is Snoopy and Woodstock. While everyone is excited about the opportunity to travel to a foreign country, Charlie is disturbed by a letter he receives from a mysterious girl from France who invites him as her guest only to find that he does not seem welcomed to her chateau.

Charlie Brown and his friends travel to Europe as exchange students.

Reviews

This 1980 charmer stands as the most enjoyable Peanuts movie to date. Chuck and his pals travel to France as foreign exchange students, where they learn of French culture, and get stranded at a spooky old French chateau. One of my favorite movies as a child stills remains dear to me today. Fans of Charlie and his pals MUST check this out. Now let's hope for a DVD release in the near future!
