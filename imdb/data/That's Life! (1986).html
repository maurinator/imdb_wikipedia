That's Life! (1986)

Summaries

Harvey and Gillian Fairchild face a very difficult weekend. Harvey, celebrating his 60th birthday, is stressed and depressed. Gillian is awaiting the results of a throat biopsy. Their lives are further complicated by their three grown children, a ditsy neighbor, a fortune teller, and an alcoholic priest.

Reviews

I found "That's Life" a really enjoyable film. Besides, there's Julie Andrews and Jack Lemmon, two wonderful actors. Try not to miss it !!!
