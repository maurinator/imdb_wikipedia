In Harm's Way (2011)

Summaries

Michael is struggling to make his mark on the world. The best thing in his life is his loyal girlfriend Shea. Outside of that he's broke with no direction or aspirations. His life changes when he becomes caught up with Ian and Tory, two old friends who are now drug dealers. They invite Michael into their world with ulterior motives but soon find themselves in a life and death situation when they must recover hundreds of thousands of dollars worth of stolen drugs for legendary international criminal & illuminati member J. Markeaur. But Markeaur has bigger problems on his hands, namely his younger brother Marko, a serial killer that Markeaur betrayed who is now hell bent on revenge. All of their activity is being monitored by a renegade cop named Kate, a mysterious woman who has her own secret agenda in seeing the demise of J. Markeaur and his organization. Their fates converge in a deadly showdown with life changing consequences. Who will live? Who will die? Regardless, they're all in harm's way.

A gritty crime drama about the interconnecting lives of drug dealers, criminals, cops, a washed up actor, a sociopath and an innocent man and woman all on a deadly collision course with each other.
