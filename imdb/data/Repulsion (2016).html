Repulsion (2016)

Summaries

Repulsion is the story of Sean B. Martin, a journalism college student who has disappeared along with his beautiful girlfriend, Rachel, somewhere in the Middle East. His desperate mother enlists the help of Michael Orwell, one of his professors to find him. And thus begins a journey into the heart of madness. Deceit, double crossings, debauchery, torture and gruesome deaths are the hallmark of life in the den of lord of terror Al Bagawi. Little does Orwell know of the horrors and surprises that await him at every turn. An exploration into the inner workings of the War on Terror and how those caught in its web struggle to find redemption, but find only Repulsion!!
