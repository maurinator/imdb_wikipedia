Running (1979)

Summaries

American marathon runner Michael Andropolis sets his heart on representing his country at the Olympic games. Meanwhile his marriage has fallen apart and his children have no respect for him. He sets out on his task with determination and hope that his success will resolve his personal problems.

Michael Andopolis has spent most of his life doing things to please other people. And the only thing that made him happy is running. His marriage is in trouble and he tries to prove his worth by trying out for the Olympics in the marathon. But he has a lot of demons to overcome.
