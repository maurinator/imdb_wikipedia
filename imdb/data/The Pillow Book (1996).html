The Pillow Book (1996)

Reviews

Erotic, seductive, intellectual, visually stunning film. The painted calligraphy on skin was amazingly sensuous. I couldn't force myself to watch '8 1/2 Women' which was my first Greenway film, so I hoped against my better judgment that this movie would be different -- and it was. I've read reviews that say this movie moves slow, but I didn't feel that at all. I think the character introspection is very important - I felt I knew Nagito. I understood her desire, her confusion and her pain. The ending is beautifully done Nagito is the perfect tragic heroine. This is a must see for anyonelooking for a sensual erotic film; unlike most you have seen before.
