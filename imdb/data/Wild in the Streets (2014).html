Wild in the Streets (2014)

Summaries

Haden makes his way out to California to spend a fun summer of extreme rollerblading with his cousin Danny, and loyal friend Sharkee. But after an unexpected run in with the villainous Hawks, Haden's plans take a turn for the worse... Naturally, the opposing forces must compete in an ultimate race for territory in this cliche-filled throw back to the extreme sports movies of the 80's and 90's. Will Haden get the girl? Will he summon the courage to defeat the Hawks? Will the inevitable training montage actually work? Find out in 'Wild in the Streets!'
