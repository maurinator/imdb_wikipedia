The Rose Seller (1998)

Summaries

13-year-old Monica leads a street life, making her living by selling flowers to couples in local nightspots, she is joined by 10-year-old Andrea who runs out of her house after her mother beats her.

Reviews

This is a movie that shows the life of a little girl who sells flowers in the streets of Medellin, Colombia. The violence, the drugs, the misery, are his partners every day.The movie is good, made with kids of the streets, there are no real actors here. The pain in the eyes of the kids is real.
