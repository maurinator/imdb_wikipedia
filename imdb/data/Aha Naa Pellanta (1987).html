Aha Naa Pellanta (1987)

Summaries

Krishnamurthy (Rajendra Prasad) is the only son of industrialist Satyanarayana (Nutan Prasad). He wants his son to get married to a girl whom he has chosen because of his bitter experience with love marriage. Before selecting the bride, Satyanaryana has a big list of conditions which need to be fulfilled before he finalizes the bride for his son. But Krishnamurthy falls in love with Padma (Rajini). After lot of tussle, both father & son enter into an understanding that if Krishnamurthy convinces Padma's father Lakshmipathy (Kota Srinivas Rao) without using his father's name by 31st December, than Satyanarayana will allow his to get married to Padma. Otherwise, he has to obey his father and marry the girl chosen by him.

Reviews

Jandhyala is a master film maker, especially in the genre of comedy. This film depicts his sense of control over comedy genre. The film is about a man trying to convince the girl's father to marry his daughter to him. Its also about a bet between the man and his father.How does he do it? Well it is best seen than explained. Sure that this one makes you roll with laughter, quite literally. The characters are so rich and deep that laughter is produced naturally. There are no efforts to create jokes, nor are there any useless jokes. Everything blends just so perfectly that you transport to a new world where everything is fun.The Classic Comedy of Telugu Cinema.
