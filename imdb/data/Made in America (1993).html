Made in America (1993)

Summaries

A young black woman discovers that her father was a sperm donor, and if that weren't bad enough, he's white.

Okay, so it's not exactly Oscar-worthy, but it's good for a few laughs.I'm a huge Whoopi fan from way back, and while this will never match Jumpin' Jack Flash or Sister Act, it's still a pretty decent film.
