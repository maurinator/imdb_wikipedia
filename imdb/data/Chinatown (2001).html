Chinatown (2001)

Summaries

On city election day, the mayor's team is exhausted. Winston insists to make his engagement with Claire public, against advice, and commits goofs which seem fatal. Staffers expect to loose their jobs, Paul even openly applies with the rival candidate. Chinatown however has its own interpretation.

On Election Day, the staff is nervous over the close race. Mayor Winston proposes to Claire (played by Farrah Fawcett) while Charlie continues his relationship with Jennifer (played by Denise Richards).
