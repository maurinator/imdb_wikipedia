A Double Life (2009)

Summaries

Shauna and Alexis could not be happier to put their high school years behind them, finally! Traveling to San Diego to attend Graham University, Shauna and Alexis couldn't wake to embark on the journey they've been forever planning. A journey that was supposed to propel the women into the actuality of their dreams will take them on a serious detour with Tyler at the helm, an experience that threatens to not only destroy their friendship, but leave them forever changed.
