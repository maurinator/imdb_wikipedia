Monster Dog (1984)

Summaries

Alice Cooper plays a pop star who takes his band to his hometown to shoot his latest video. They are greeted by the sheriff (whom he's known since a kid) that warn him that there's been several murders in the area where the bodies were ripped to shreds. The police think it's a pack of wild dogs, but Alice knows differnt.

A rock star returns to his childhood home to shoot a music video while a pack of wild dogs are on the loose killing off the local residents.

Reviews

Monster Dog was Atmospheric,creepy, and contained some good claustrophobic settings. The music score was also scary, and the pace was excellent. Never boring, and fun to watch. Check it out on a rainy day.
