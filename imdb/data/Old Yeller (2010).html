Old Yeller (2010)

Summaries

Lane Hick is a young, bright Hollywood assistant who works for a sleazy but succesful reality show producer. His tasks are usually inane and degrading, but reach absurdity when the boss asks Lane to have his dog euthanized. When Lane finds out the dog isn't sick, he has to struggle with the moral dilemma of killing an innocent animal to advance his career in Hollywood.
