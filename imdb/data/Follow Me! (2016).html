Follow Me! (2016)

Summaries

Destiny changing journey on earth took Girl From Space from Minsk to Changchun, Kaliningrad, Copenhagen, Guangzhou, Shanghai, Beijing, Macao, Hong Kong, Los Angeles, New York City, San Francisco, Portland, Seattle and Washington DC. Her earth experience is fascinating. She creates images of people and places them in a Blue Bag Follow Me! She exclaims: 'Fly! Blue Bag Follow Me, to politicians, businesspersons, artists, publishers, accountants, art directors, rehabilitation centers, lawyers, actors, kings, no limits! To children, ballet dancers, professors, athletes, thinkers, prisoners, big bosses, to you!' The mission of the Blue Bag Follow Me is to connect dreamers with Girl From Space. Local hosts in cities around the world help Girl From Space to reach dreamers. The dreamers have two attempts to touch a heart of Girl From Space. They describe the art cards, share their big dreams, challenges and give Girl From Space a departing messaging. In the future Girl From Space will reach them again to see what happened to them on the ways to their dreams.
