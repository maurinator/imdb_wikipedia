Superstar (2010)

Summaries

In 2007, a failed heavy weight boxer and his girlfriend are on the run after attacking an innocent man in Louisiana. High on drugs and looking for cash, they go on a multi-state rampage picking out victims at random and bulldozing their way through town after town. As they reach the north, with 4 jurisdictions after them, a huge manhunt ensues, but will they catch them before they meet their next victim?

Wicked Attraction looks at the case of former boxer Jeremy Brooks and Coty Rodriguez who commit three murders in three different states.
