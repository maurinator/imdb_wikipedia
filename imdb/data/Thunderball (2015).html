Thunderball (2015)

Summaries

This week, it's the film infamous for being tangled in a decade-long string of script treatments, legal disputes, and inevitably the death of Ian Fleming himself. What movie could possibly cause all this mayhem and despair? It's the one, the only, THUNDERBALL. SPECTRE's Number Two, Emilio Largo, has stolen two atomic bombs, and is holding Miami for a million-pound ransom. Can Bond locate the bombs before Florida is annihilated?
