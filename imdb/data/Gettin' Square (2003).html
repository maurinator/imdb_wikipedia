Gettin' Square (2003)

Summaries

Gettin' Square is about starting over, keeping clean and going straight. Barry Wirth is fresh out of prison and determined to stay on the straight and narrow. But like his mate Johnny 'Spit' Spiteri and reformed gangster turned restaurateur Dabba, he finds out the hard way that there are old scores and a few new ones that'll make getting square a lot harder than he thought.

Barry 'Wattsy' Wirth was a model prisoner and is determined to make a fresh start, far from crime. He proves himself as a cook. But both his old crime partners and the dirty cop who caused him being jailed as a scape-goat and harassed him in prison push and prod for Wattsy to put his criminal talent to profitable use again for them. Once they have enlist his gullible adolescent brother Joey and dumb mate Johnny Francis 'Spit' Spitieri, Barry accepts. But this time he has his own agenda, and it's full of smashing surprises.
