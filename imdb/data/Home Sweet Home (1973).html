Home Sweet Home (1973)

Summaries

In einem Brüsseler Altersheim lehnen sich die Bewohner mit Unterstützung von Pflegerin Claire und Sozialhelfer Jacques gegegn die Bevormundung durch die Direktion auf. Ein ernstes und ernst zu nehmendes Thema in einer ausgewogenen Mischung aus Humor und sozialkritischem Engagement. Spezialpreis in Moskau 1973.

Reviews

I saw this ages ago, and it flashed into my mind one day so I thought I'd look it up on IMDb. When I saw it, it was a supporting film for a Claude Lelouch film, and I was expecting nothing from it. I agree with the previous comment (Jefke Peeters) (which now no longer seems to be here). It was a surprise and a delight from start to finish: consistently inventive, funny and charming. Unfortunately it seems to be one of those films that made little impact when released and sank quickly without trace. It would be lovely for somebody to find it and bring it out for everyone to enjoy again. The Lelouch film was good, too, as it happens, but this is the one that I remember.
