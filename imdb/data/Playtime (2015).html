Playtime (2015)

Summaries

Playtime follows the path of Logan, a troubled teen with a mysterious past. We watch Logan be transported into a new life to which she must adjust. Logan joins the Drama Club at her new school, and we find she is a powerful stage actress. After being cast as the lead in the school's play, Logan begins seeing things she can't explain. As rehearsals progress, the eerie happenings begin to affect the other actors, and we learn more about Logan's past. In an escalation of events, the play's director disappears before the final rehearsal. Performance night carries a certain mounting tension and ends with a rather violent bang, drawing close the curtains on this act of Logan's life.
