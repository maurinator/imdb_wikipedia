Bus Stop (1985)

Summaries

Natalie graduates from high school and her family wants her to go to college. But she wants to go out and see the world. So to avoid having to tell everyone what she wants to do, she decides to apply to a college which she thinks will not accept her. But to her surprise she is.

When Natalie graduates from Eastland, she shocks her mother and her friends by deciding not to go to college.
