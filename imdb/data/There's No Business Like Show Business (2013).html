There's No Business Like Show Business (2013)

Summaries

Sandy does damage control after her disastrous interview and Will's abrupt departure; Mary Esther tries to force Herbert to let her in his Christmas show this year; Nipsey gives relationship advice to an estranged Dick and Lorraine; Not being able to stand the thought of leaving showbusiness for good, Frank seizes an opportunity to make Julia a star; Trisha and Heidi try to figure out what they want to do with their lives.
