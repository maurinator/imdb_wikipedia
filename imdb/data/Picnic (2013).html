Picnic (2013)

Summaries

The year is 2017. Zombies infest the lands. Survivors stay in doors. But, as for Jay and Cecilia, it's their one-year wedding anniversary. Jay plans a picnic outside the infected zones and despite Cecilia's traumatic past, Jay convinces her that this is something they both need. Against her intuition, she agrees. The picnic is nothing less than perfect, until a Zombie Horde arrives. Outnumbered, the couple is forced into the maze filled woods and must do anything to survive. Picnic is an action packed horror-fest filled with twisted zombies and unexpected surprises.
