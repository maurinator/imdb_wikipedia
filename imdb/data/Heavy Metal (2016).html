Heavy Metal (2016)

Summaries

Heavy Metal is a short horror film based on a segment of Betty Dravis's Book, Six Pack of Blood and Co-Authored by Barbara Watkins. What happens when you crank up the volume and listen to Heavy Metal? What did your parents always say would happen if you did? Consider the most amazing and dreadful consequences that will blow your mind when you see this film!

What happens when your mother tells you not to listen to Heavy Metal, but you do it anyway...and then you bring your friends in on this mischievous deed as well! Beware the consequences of what mother says!
