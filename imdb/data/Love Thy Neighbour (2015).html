Love Thy Neighbour (2015)

Summaries

An inspiring behind-the-scenes documentary about the Love Thy Neighbour 2014 community event hosted by a local church in Perth, Western Australia. The film covers the one year lead up of the event as the camera follows the event organisers and interviews the team leaders, volunteers and local government who came together to make Love Thy Neighbour 2014 possible. The result is an inspiring, uplifting and visionary story of hope and the start of a powerful movement.
