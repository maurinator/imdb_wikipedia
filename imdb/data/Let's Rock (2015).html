Let's Rock (2015)

Summaries

This morning Uncle Bob has a meeting with his old friends. Audrey, Garbour and the little ghosts find a poster and understand that some time ago Uncle Bob used to play in a rock band. They find the instruments of his band in the attic and they decide to make their own rock band. But how? Flim Flam can play a piano, because he took lessons from the most famous ghost musician when he was little. Garbour took guitar lessons from Uncle Bob. Squeak masters the drums very quickly. And Boom starts with the double bass. Audrey writes the lyrics of the song. In the evening, when Uncle Bob comes home, they play him their song.
