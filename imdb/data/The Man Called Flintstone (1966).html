The Man Called Flintstone (1966)

Summaries

In this feature-length film based on the "Flintstones" TV show, secret agent Rock Slag is injured during a chase in Bedrock. Slag's chief decides to replace the injured Slag with Fred Flintstone, who just happens to look like him. The trip takes Fred to Paris and Rome, which is good for Wilma, Barney, and Betty-but can Fred foil the mysterious Green Goose's evil plan for a destructive missile without letting his wife and friends in on his secret?

Fred Flintstone was about to go on vacation with his family and his best friend, Barney Rubble and his family. But when he gets in accident, he goes to the hospital to be checked out. Also there is an agent named Rock Slagg who looks like him, who survived an attempt made on him by the men of a criminal known as the Green Goose. . HIs chief wants him to go to Paris and get the Green Goose. But before he can go, the Green Goose's men injure Slagg so he can't go on the mission. But when the Chief sees Fred and sees how much he looks like Slagg, he asks him to go to Paris on the mission but not telling him the full extent of the mission. He tells Fred he can bring his whole family along. Fred decides to bring Barney and his family along. And when they get there the Green Goose's men see him thinking he's Slagg tries to get rid of him but fail constantly. And Fred learns being a secret agent isn't easy especially when he has to lie to Wilma.

Reviews

This movie based on the famous TV series may be one of the best annimated movies ever made. It is a spoof of James Bond movies of the era. The script is well written with an interesting story line and an exciting ending. The musical numbers are fun too. There is a song that Pebbles & Bamm-Bamm sing where they are dreaming about their futures and the visuals during the song are animated in stick figures that look to be drawn by kids. This has to be the cutest song and animation that I have ever seen.
