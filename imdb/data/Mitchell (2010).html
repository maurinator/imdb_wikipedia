Mitchell (2010)

Summaries

Brady, Carlyle, Scott, and Kasey Mitchell still live together in the same gated community McMansion that claimed their parents' lives a decade earlier. Following in their parents' footsteps, they have each become lawyers and though they excel professionally, the world that waits for them outside of their offices is a strange and foreign one. When Brady mistakenly believes he's been registered in a nonexistent military draft, his family rushes to defend him from the horrors of a life in service using any means necessary.

A half hour comedy about four socially inept siblings, still living under the same roof, clawing their way from upper middle class to lower upper class.
