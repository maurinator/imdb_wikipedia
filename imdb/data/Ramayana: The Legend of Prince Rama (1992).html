Ramayana: The Legend of Prince Rama (1992)

Summaries

An anime adaptation of the Hindu myth the Ramayana, where the avatar Sri Ram combats the wicked king Ravana.

Reviews

I was 11 or 12 when i first watch this movie on TV.And i was like WOW. i have already watched the Ramayana series by Ramanand Sagar which is an epic, but it had told the story in a number of episodes... while here we got the main story of prince Rama in 170 minute, although it didn't cover the whole epic but it still amazes me.the voice over actors did their best especially Amrish Puri, He had done some very memorable villainous roles in some of the best Hindi movies and i think its one of them. While Arun Govil has Played Rama in Ramamnand Sagar's Ramayana that's why his voice seems very original to me and to many of the other people who has seen the TV series.And now if we talk about the animation...its marvelous if we compare it with the time it was produced and especially in India. where there is no animation movie been release before. this movie has give me some best memories of my life.that is why i still remembers it. And i m 19 years old now.
