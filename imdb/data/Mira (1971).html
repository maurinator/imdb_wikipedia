Mira (1971)

Summaries

The story of a small and conservative West-Flemish village opposing the construction of a bridge over the Scheldt.

Reviews

Willeke van Ammelrooy in her first big movie that also made her a Dutch star. She later made the Oscar winning movie Antonia (1995). Director Fons Rademaker also won an Oscar for De aanslag. This movie is known for it sexy appearance of van Ammelrooy. It was the start of the dutch film revival
