The Great Escape (1995)

Summaries

On Monday morning with Mandy and Martin at work Matthew enacts his film role fantasies and tries unsuccessfully to get the girl next door to come in. In the evening he threatens to evict Mandy but she tells him she will probably be leaving anyway, having met a millionaire who has offered her a job in Bermuda. She returns from her date to tell Martin she thinks the man was a liar who got drunk and passed out. Martin confesses his own insecurity but they are interrupted by a paranoid Matthew who accuses them of plotting against him and tells them to leave immediately. However it turns out that this is just something he does every Monday night and he quickly backs down.
