Giant (1985)

Summaries

This video contains full footage of some of Andre The Giant's matches from the 70's and early 80's, mixed with an appearance of his on the WWF show Tuesday Night Titans. The video is narrated by Lord Alfred Hayes. Matches Include: Andre vs Rex Moondog, 20 Man Battle Royale, Andre vs Black Gordman & The Great Goliath, Andre vs Gorilla Monsoon (boxing match), Andre & Jimmy 'Superfly' Snuka vs The Wild Samoans, Andre vs The Masked Superstar, Andre & SD Jones vs Ken Patera & Big John Studd, Andre vs Ken Patera, Andre vs Big John Studd(Wrestlemania I)
