Stay Tuned (2009)

Summaries

The girls, other Playmates, and Hef appear in a commercial for "Guitar Hero 5" filmed at the Mansion. Later, Crystal goes to San Diego to tell her mom, Lee, that she is taking singing lessons. Lee tells Crystal about when she was in a band with Crystal's dad (who died a few years back) and plays her a tape of one of her dad's songs. Later, the girls go to Mary O'Connor's house to have dinner with her and her friends. Crystal tells them of her desire to record her dad's song to give to her mom, prompting Frances, one of Mary's friends, to tell Crystal that she is married to Smokey Robinson. Later, Smokey himself comes to a recording studio to listen to Crystal sing. He tells Crystal that her singing is not bad and that she should believe in herself and keep it up. After Crystal completes her recording of her dad's song, Lee comes to the Mansion, and Crystal arranges to have the song played as they watch a montage of their family photos.

After Crystal takes singing lessons, she decides to record a song written by her late father as a gift to her mom.
