Fame (2010)

Summaries

Jimmy Jones, a young and adoring fan, outs Ben's superhero secret on the Internet, which popular entertainment figures subsequently use to present Ben as a menace to society. Still, Jimmy's compiled footage reveals a problem at NASA, where an unstoppable alien (of a species not even recognized by the Ultimatrix) has nightly and bit by bit been dismantling NASA's latest project. Ben, Gwen and Kevin must stop it before it sets off the project's power source - a nuclear bomb.

Ben's secret is out. As Ben deals with his newfound fame, a new threat emerges as a thief steals parts of a new advanced spaceship from NASA.
