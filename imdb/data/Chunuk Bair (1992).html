Chunuk Bair (1992)

Summaries

A New Zealand regiment is ordered to take the hill Chunuk Bair, without the promised British support . An operation without sense , as they must return to the bottom. It is when a British ship opened fire on the hill.

Reviews

This is a fantastic film. The drama involved made the film realistic and life-like sequences really make you fell sorry for the poor diggers and their experiences. The ending is particularly sad, All in all a great film.
