Charlotte's Web (2016)

Summaries

The girls are forced to stay in Rosewood as questions about the new mystery surface; Aria starts to look suspicious as she heads back to Boston early; Spencer, Emily and Hanna find themselves back in familiar territory.

Alison invites all her friends for dinner, except Aria, who's left the town. Aria comes clean about the night of Charlotte's murder. New things are revealed concerning Emily. Spencer and Caleb start flirting.
