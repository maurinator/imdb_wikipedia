Clockwise (2013)

Summaries

Clockwise is the story of Ed, an everyman trapped by his routine who realizes his life could be much more exciting. Every single day he wakes up at the same time and does the same activities. However, one night Ed dreams he is in a park with plentiful nature. For the first time in his life he feels free. But something happens. He sees a strange figure sitting on a bench along the lake. Who is him? The man turns around and the vision is unbelievable. Ed finds out himself, wearing a suit and showing up a clock. What a shock! He wakes up astonished and looks at his ringing alarm. Affected by the dream, Ed decides to turn it off and stay on bed.
