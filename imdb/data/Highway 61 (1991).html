Highway 61 (1991)

Summaries

A naive Canadian barber who knows US popular culture inside and out meets a flamboyant roadie who needs someone to drive her and her "brother's" corpse to New Orleans. Chaos ensues after the barber agrees to drive her, the corpse, and the drugs stashed within all the way.

Reviews

Surprisingly had only two written comments on this film, and then I can't help myself! This film has got some scenes which you'll never forget. A road movie with everything you want from one of it's kind. It all starts off when a local Canadian barber one morning is asked by a strange woman if he can drive her and her dead brother down into the U.S. - But is it her dead brother??? On the way they meet all kinds of strange people. This film contains so much - like the most fantastic bingo-scene in film ever, and what about indoor chicken hunt with guns, or the dream of having U2 to play in your wedding! I can't wait to see this movie again, and I understand it's now on DVD. So go buy it, never mind the price! This is hilarious fun with death on the back wheels! Don't miss it! The best Canadian film I ever saw!
