Second Sight (1989)

Summaries

John Larroquette is the head of a detective agency that is hired to find a missing person, probably kidnapped. They employ the help of a mystic/psychic to help find the missing girl.

Reviews

Our first time seeing Bronson Pinchot, who was brilliant. All together a very funny film.We haven't seen it since it's release but plan on renting it to enjoy once more. That's something that we rarely do.
