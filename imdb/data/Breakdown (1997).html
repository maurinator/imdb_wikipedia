Breakdown (1997)

Summaries

During school, Beavis and Butt-head's uncontrollable laughter finally sends Princpal McVicker to the Mental Institution. Coach Buzzcut is dead set on kicking their asses for what they've done and will not let some stupid hippie stop him. At the mental institution, Van Driessen tries to get the boys to apologize to McVicker for what happened. They also end up driving their teacher to the brink of insanity. He is spared from it by Coach Buzzcut who followed them here. Their laughing doesn't affect the former marine unlike Van Driessen and McVicker. He ends up kicking Beavis and Butt-Head's asses for what happened. Then he grabs them and forces them to apologize the principal at once. Later, he turns to Van Driessen and adamantly tells him that this is why the majority of students and the faculty hates them so much.

Beavis and Butt-head finally send Principal McVicker straight into a mental institution... as if that wasn't inevitable.
