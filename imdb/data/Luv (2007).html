Luv (2007)

Summaries

Lily's and Tok Kong's marriage hits a glitch when Lily becomes jealous that newlyweds Dollah and Haslinda are more romantic than them. But Dollah and Haslinda have been hiding a secret - they have not consummated their love yet. Meanwhile, Xiao Long, starts a bogus podcast on love and relationships, calling himself "Dr. Luv", which brings disastrous consequences to everyone.
